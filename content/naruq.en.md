## About the Foundation

Private Foundation "NAR-UQ" is a non-profit organization established under the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

The Foundation has no membership and does not pursue profit-making goals. All activities are aimed at achieving social, educational, cultural, and scientific objectives.

## Main Goals

Implementation of scientific, educational, and analytical activities, development and introduction of innovations contributing to cultural, humanitarian, and intellectual development.

## Areas of Activity

- Implementation of research projects and programs in fundamental and applied research
- Research on issues in humanitarian, intellectual, and cultural development with recommendations and expert opinions
- Organizing international conferences, symposiums, seminars, and round tables
- Publishing and distributing scientific, educational, informational, and analytical materials
- Scientific, analytical, and expert activities
