## Ontology of Nothing

A philosophical monograph deriving the structure of being from the concept of absolute immutability. 28 chapters, 4 parts. Solves the classical aporia: how does changeable reality arise from an immutable foundation.

**Status:** completed (Russian)

---

## The Proto-Semitic Root *ʔil-

A monograph on Semitic linguistics. Corpus analysis of five Semitic languages (Eblaite, Ugaritic, Hebrew, Aramaic, Arabic). Argumentation against traditional etymologies proposing meanings of "strength" or "primacy".

**Status:** completed (Russian), being prepared for Q1 publication
