## The Proto-Semitic Root *Ê”il-

A monograph on Semitic linguistics. Corpus analysis of five Semitic languages: Eblaite, Ugaritic, Hebrew, Aramaic, Arabic.

Argumentation against traditional etymologies proposing meanings of "strength" or "primacy". The original meaning "progenitor/initiator" is proposed.

**Status:** completed (Russian), being prepared for Q1 publication
